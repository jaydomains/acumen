### Autonomy mechanisms

- **A-9 — Provenance chain spec.** An end-to-end **provenance chain**: corpus source → generation
  (prompt_version / model / tokens / cost) → cross-model verification → auto-publish decision —
  recorded so every auto-published pill is traceable to its sources and the confidence/verification
  that cleared it. *Measured against:* AC-CD8 (`prompt_version` persisted; `catalogue.py:533`),
  `AIResult` provenance struct (`provider.py:167-184`); the autonomy accountability requirement
  (provenance is the audit substrate **because** the human gate is gone).
- **A-10 — Single GLOBAL confidence threshold (not per-type) — ratified counter to recommendation.**
  The plan must specify **one** global confidence threshold governing auto-publish — **not**
  per-type / per-operation thresholds — **and** must record this as **explicitly ratified against a
  contrary recommendation**: surface the recommendation (presumably per-type), note the spec author
  overrode it, and cite the ratification key. A plan that silently bakes a single threshold *without*
  recording the override, or that drifts to per-type, fails this. *Measured against:* the ratification
  list; role files §6 (faithful recording, push-back adjudicated against the spec, not persistence).
- **A-11 — Cross-model verification protocol.** A **different-provider verification** step gating
  auto-publish — extending the AC-D19 cross-family review + AC-D23 anchor self-review pattern
  (Anthropic-generated → OpenAI-verified, or equivalent) to autonomous publishing. *Measured against:*
  AC-D19 (`DECISIONS.md:21`/§6.6), AC-D23 step 2 (§6.7); the reframe (verification replaces the human
  reviewer). Must state what is verified, the pass/fail action, and the fail-soft posture.
- **A-12 — Full rollback matrix: pill + question + batch + source.** The plan must specify rollback
  across **all four** dimensions — individual **pill**, individual **question**, **batch** (a whole
  generation run), and **source** (a corpus source + everything derived from it, transitively). A
  missing dimension is a finding; **source-level** rollback is the load-bearing one (a bad corpus
  source contaminates everything downstream irreversibly without it). *Measured against:* the autonomy
  reframe (auto-publish demands reversibility); AC-D14 (pill retire) + AC-D17 (forward-only edit)
  precedents.

  *Measured against:* role files §8.3 (supersede-by-recency; inferred-authority pending),
